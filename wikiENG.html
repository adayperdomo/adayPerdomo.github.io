Welcome to the Sekiro wiki


Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is an action-adventure video game developed by FromSoftware and published by Activision. Directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki; it was released on March 22, 2019 for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Microsoft Windows. Its story features a shinobi named Sekiro, whose aim is to seek revenge on a samurai who attacked him and kidnapped his lord.

Set in the late years of the Sengoku period; Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice depicts a withered yet vibrant world, created through a reimagining of Japanese-inspired aesthetics; where a lone shinobi battles, bound by code. Freely traverse meticulously designed open three-dimensional environments, brimming with secrets to explore, as you use everything at your disposal to face powerful enemies in this truly unique experience.
