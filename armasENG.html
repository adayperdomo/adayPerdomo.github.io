Main weapons

Kusabimaru

Description

Katana received from Kuro, the Celestial Descendant. An inheritance from the Hirata family, one of the branches of the Ashina family. Although she was believed lost, she has returned to the hands of the Wolf. The name Kusabimaru appeals to the fact that "the function of the shinobi is to kill, but not even a shinobi should forget compassion." A mantra that the sword itself can manifest.

Espada Mortal

Description

An odachi capable of killing those who cannot die. Its crimson blade will take the life of whoever draws it. Only he with the power of resurrection could wield such a terrible weapon, hidden for years in the Senpo temple. The sword is inscribed with its true name: "Refined Gift of Tears."
